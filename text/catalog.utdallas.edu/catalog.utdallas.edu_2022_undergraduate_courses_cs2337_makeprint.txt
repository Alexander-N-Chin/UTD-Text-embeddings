UT Dallas 2022 Undergraduate CatalogCS2337 - Computer Science IICS 2337 Computer Science II (3 semester credit hours) Further applications of programming techniques, introducing the fundamental concepts of data structures and algorithms. Topics include recursion, fundamental data structures (including stacks, queues, linked lists, hash tables, trees, and graphs), and algorithmic analysis. Includes comprehensive programming projects. Programming language of choice is C++. Credit cannot be received for both CS 2337 and (CS 2336 or CE 2336). Prerequisite: AP score of at least 4. Prerequisite or Corequisite: (CE 2305 or CS 2305) with a grade of C or better or (Data Science major and MATH 3315). (3-0) S
