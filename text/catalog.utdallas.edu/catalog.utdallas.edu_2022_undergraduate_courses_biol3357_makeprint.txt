UT Dallas 2022 Undergraduate CatalogBIOL3357 - Mammalian Physiology with LabBIOL 3357 Mammalian Physiology with Lab (3 semester credit hours) This course will focus on human body systems and physiological pathways related to organ system functions and control including, but not limited to, central nervous system control and feedback, cardiovascular, respiratory, and neuromuscular physiology as well as topics such as blood pressure regulation and exercise physiology. This course will use computer software and electronic instrumentation for performing electrocardiography, electromyography, electroencephalography, plethysmography, pulmonary function analysis, polygraph analysis, and biofeedback. Instructor consent required. Prerequisites: BIOL 3455 or equivalent and BIOL 3456 or equivalent. (3-1) S
