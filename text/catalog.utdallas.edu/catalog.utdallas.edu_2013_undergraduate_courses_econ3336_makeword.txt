UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogECON3336 - Economics of EducationECON 3336 Economics of Education (3 semester hours) This course looks at education through the lens of economics. Topics include ways to finance education, various controversies in the production of human capital, public policies that are designed to improve education and the consequences of poor educational performance. Prerequisite: ECON 2302. (3-0) R
