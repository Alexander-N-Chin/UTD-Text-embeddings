UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogLANG2311 - Intermediate Language Instruction ILANG 2311 Intermediate Language Instruction I (3 semester hours) This course is a continuation of beginning instruction in foreign languages not taught on a regular basis. The course integrates acquisition of language skills (listening, speaking, reading, and writing) with study of culture and civilization. Languages will vary but may include Italian, Hindi, or Russian. May be repeated as language varies (6 hours maximum). Prerequisite: LANG 1312 or equivalent or instructor consent required.
