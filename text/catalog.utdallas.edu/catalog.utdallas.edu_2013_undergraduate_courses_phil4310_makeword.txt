UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogPHIL4310 - Philosophy of TechnologyPHIL 4310 Philosophy of Technology (3 semester hours) An examination of the nature of technology and its role in personal life and society. Focus on the conceptualization of technology, the relation of science to technology, the impact of technology on science and ethics, and the influence of technology on culture. Prerequisite: PHIL 1301 or PHIL 2316 or PHIL 2317 or instructor consent required. (3-0) Y
