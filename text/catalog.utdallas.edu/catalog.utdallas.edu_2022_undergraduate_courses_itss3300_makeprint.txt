UT Dallas 2022 Undergraduate CatalogITSS3300 - Information Technology for BusinessITSS 3300 Information Technology for Business (3 semester credit hours) Examines key business processes in organizations and how information systems support the execution and management of these processes. The course focuses on using information technology and information systems to support decision-making, thus blending technical and managerial topics. Students will be exposed to principles of information technology and information systems and work directly with a variety of information systems tools and techniques such as Excel, Tableau, and relational database management systems.
