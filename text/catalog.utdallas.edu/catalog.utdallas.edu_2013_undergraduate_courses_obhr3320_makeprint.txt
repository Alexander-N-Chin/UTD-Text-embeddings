UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogOBHR3320 - Groups and TeamsOBHR 3320 Groups and Teams (3 semester hours) This course focuses on how groups and teams can be used to maximize organizational success. Students will be introduced to theories and concepts that will allow them to analyze and manage groups in organizations. Topics will include building teams, managing teams, the opportunities and challenges of diversity in teams, managing conflict, and leadership. Practical experience will be developed through the use of exercises, case-studies, and the completion of a team project. Prerequisites: OBHR 3300 and OBHR 3310. (3-0) T
