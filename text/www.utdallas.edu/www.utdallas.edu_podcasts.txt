UT Dallas CometCast Network
Podcasts for Comets and ponderers alike.
The CometCast Network is brought to you by The University of Texas at Dallas. The podcasts are created by various departments and programs throughout the University. The viewpoints and opinions expressed in these podcasts do not represent the views The University of Texas at Dallas.





The Ackerman Center Podcast
The Ackerman Center Podcast provides a space for thoughtful and in-depth conversation about the Holocaust, genocide, and human rights studies. Launched in 2020 by a team of dedicated faculty and research assistants, this podcast works to extend the conversation beyond academic audiences—providing an educational, engaging, and transformative experience.
Anchor | Google | Pocket Casts | Radio Public | Spotify







The Atheneaum Review
The Athenaeum Review podcast features interviews with leading thinkers about arts and ideas.
Apple | Spotify







Could We Ever…?
A podcast that shines a light on UT Dallas experts and asks them to tackle questions you never knew you needed answered — from science to art and more.
Anchor | Google | Overcast | Pocket Casts | Radio Public | Spotify | Stitcher | YouTube







Comets Discuss
Conversations with UT Dallas faculty and staff about current events and issues.
Anchor | Apple | Google | Overcast | Pocket Casts | Radio Public | Spotify | YouTube







Decade of Excellence: Re-imagining Human Health
“Decade of excellence: Re-Imagining human health.” is a Podcast series part of the 10th Anniversary celebration for the Bioengineering department. Through this series, Dr. Shalini Prasad will be talking with multiple invitees about the department’s genesis, present, and future.
Anchor | Apple | Google | Spotify | YouTube







The Business of Healthcare Podcast
The Business of Healthcare Podcast is presented by the Center for Healthcare Leadership and Management. It brings together business leaders and other forward thinkers to discuss how best to meet the challenges of a rapidly changing, increasingly complex healthcare industry. The center is based at the Naveen Jindal School of Management at The University of Texas at Dallas.
Deezer | Apple | TuneIn | Stitcher | Google | Overcast | Pocket Casts | Spotify | YouTube







Translating The World
The podcast highlights translators who give presence to foreign writers in English, interviews with writers and their translators, portraits of contemporary international writers, and bilingual readings of poetry. The Podcast is for people who are interested in international literature and cultures. A podcast of the Center for Translation Studies at the University of Texas at Dallas created and produced by Sarah Valente.
Anchor | Apple | Google | Overcast | Pocket Casts | Radio Public | Spotify







Pep Talk
Conversations on Policy, Economics, and Politics. We are a student-run podcast brought to you by the School of Economic, Political, and Policy Sciences at The University of Texas at Dallas.
Anchor | Apple | Google | Overcast | Pocket Casts | Radio Public | Stitcher | Spotify







UT Dallas Through the Years
A look at the first 50 years of The University of Texas at Dallas from the people who were there.
