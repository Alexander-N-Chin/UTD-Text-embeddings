UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogCRWT4354 - Creating Play, Movie, and Television ScriptsCRWT 4354 Creating Play, Movie, and Television Scripts (3 semester hours) An advanced workshop on the aesthetics, art, and creation of play, movie, and television scripts which will focus on the creative techniques and the creative process involved not only in the creation of film, play, and television scripts, but also in the production of plays, films, and television episodes. May be repeated for credit as topics vary (9 hours maximum). Prerequisite: CRWT 2301 or instructor consent required. (3-0) T
