UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogDANC3347 - Dance CompositionDANC 3347 Dance Composition (3 semester hours) Students will study basic concepts and applications for dance composition at a beginning level. Principles and skills will be taught through projects, analysis, and the creation of a complete work. May be repeated for credit (9 hours maximum). Prerequisite: DANC 3332 or DANC 3333 or DANC 3334 or DANC 3335 or instructor consent required. (0-3) Y
