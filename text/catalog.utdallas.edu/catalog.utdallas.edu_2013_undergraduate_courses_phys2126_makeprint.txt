UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogPHYS2126 - Physics Laboratory IIPHYS 2126 (PHYS 2126) Physics Laboratory II (1 semester hour) Laboratory course to accompany any Physics II or Electricity and Magnetism course. Experiments investigate Coulomb's Law, electric fields, Ohm's and Kirchoff's laws, RC circuits, magnetic forces between conductors, motors, and transformers. Corequisite: PHYS 1302 or PHYS 2326 or PHYS 2422. (0-3) S
