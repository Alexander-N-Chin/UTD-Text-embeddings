UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogACCT3332 - Intermediate Financial Accounting IIACCT 3332 Intermediate Financial Accounting II (3 semester hours) This course is a continuation of topics in external financial reporting including issues related to the measurement and reporting of current liabilities and contingencies, bonds, leases, deferred taxes, pensions, stock-based compensation plans, stockholders equity, earnings per share, accounting changes, and cash flows. Current generally accepted accounting principles for financial reporting are analyzed. Prerequisite: ACCT 3331 with a minimum grade of C. (3-0) S
