UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogPSCI4357 - Human Rights and the Rule of the LawPSCI 4357 Human Rights and the Rule of the Law (3 semester hours) This course focuses on the development of norms involving international human rights and law as well as major and competing theories that sometimes weigh against the development of universal human rights. Also examines the effectiveness of the courts and law, including international courts and truth commissions, in the area of human rights. (3-0) R
