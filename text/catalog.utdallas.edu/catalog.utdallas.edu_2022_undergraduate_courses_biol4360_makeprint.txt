UT Dallas 2022 Undergraduate CatalogBIOL4360 - Evolution and DevelopmentBIOL 4360 Evolution and Development (3 semester credit hours) The objective of the course is to integrate evolutionary biology and developmental biology into a common framework, focusing on the evolution of developmental pathways as a basis for the evolution of animal morphology. This is a reading intensive course with a heavy focus on scientific research. Prerequisite or Corequisite: BIOL 3301. (3-0) S
