UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogMIS3320 - Business Analytics Using ExcelMIS 3320 Business Analytics Using Excel (3 semester hours) A focus on practical IT skills required to model and solve business problems. It is an interdisciplinary course that draws upon problems from accounting, finance, marketing, production, and human resource fields. The course emphasizes learning-by-doing using Excel and its add-ons as the primary tool. Students taking this class are required to have a laptop. Prerequisite: MIS 3300. (3-0) Y
