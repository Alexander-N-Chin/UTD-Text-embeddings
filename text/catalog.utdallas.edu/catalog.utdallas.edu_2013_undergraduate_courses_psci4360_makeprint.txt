UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogPSCI4360 - The Political Economy of Multinational CorporationsPSCI 4360 The Political Economy of Multinational Corporations (3 semester hours) In addition to the historical rise of international firms, the course covers the economic theory of the firm, multinational corporations (MNCs) as political actors, the dynamics of foreign direct investment, and the relationship of MNCs to developing countries. The aim of the course is to understand the causes and effects of the behavior of transnational corporations, particularly in regard to economic policy. All students are required to complete assignments that fulfill the University's writing requirement. (3-0) T
