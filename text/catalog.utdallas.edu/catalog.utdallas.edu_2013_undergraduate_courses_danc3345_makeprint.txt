UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogDANC3345 - Dance PerformanceDANC 3345 Dance Performance (3 semester hours) Exploration of various choreographic styles and ideas of performance. Emphasis may be placed on the application of dance techniques in choreographed works. Methods may focus on the choreographic process to enrich the performer's range of technique and expression and encourage understanding of choreographic principles and practices. Audition may be required for enrollment in this course. May be repeated for credit (9 hours maximum). Prerequisite: Instructor consent required. (0-3) T
