UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogDRAM2371 - Technical Theater 1DRAM 2371 Technical Theater 1 (3 semester hours) Designed to provide an introductory overview of all aspects of technical theater, encompassing lighting, sound, set construction, props, costume procedures, and elementary construction. Practical work will reference theater history and the theoretical foundations of technical theater. Students will be required to attend weekly lectures and fulfill production lab hours. (0-3) Y
