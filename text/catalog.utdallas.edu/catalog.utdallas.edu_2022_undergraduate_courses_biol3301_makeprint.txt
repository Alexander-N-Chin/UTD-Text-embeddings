UT Dallas 2022 Undergraduate CatalogBIOL3301 - Classical and Molecular GeneticsBIOL 3301 Classical and Molecular Genetics (3 semester credit hours) The phenomenon of heredity, its cytological and molecular basis; gene expression and transfer of genetic information, with major focus on bacterial and model eukaryotic systems; genetic recombination and chromosome mapping; tetrad analysis; mutations and mutagenesis; genetic interactions; application of recombinant DNA techniques to genetic analysis. Prerequisites: BIOL 2311 and (BIOL 2281 or CHEM 2401 or equivalent) and (CHEM 2323 or equivalent). Corequisite: BIOL 3101. (3-0) S
