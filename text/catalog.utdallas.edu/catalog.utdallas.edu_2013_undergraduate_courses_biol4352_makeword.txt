UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogBIOL4352 - Medical Molecular and Cell BiologyBIOL 4352 Medical Molecular and Cell Biology (3 semester hours) Topics related to health and disease will be examined from a molecular and cellular perspective. Topics will vary but will be selected from new and developing applications of cell biology to cancer, heart disease, fat metabolism, mitochondrial disorders, aging, Alzheimer's, etc. Students are expected to participate actively in discussions and make an oral presentation. Prerequisite: BIOL 3302. (3-0) T
