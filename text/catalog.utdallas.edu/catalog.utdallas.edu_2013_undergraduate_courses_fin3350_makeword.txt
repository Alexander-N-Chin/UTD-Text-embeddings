UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogFIN3350 - Financial Markets and InstitutionsFIN 3350 Financial Markets and Institutions (3 semester hours) Examines the operation of financial markets and financial intermediaries, along with their role in providing financing to the public and private sectors. Topics covered include the banking system, markets for short-term securities, financial derivatives, and market for foreign exchange. Prerequisite or corequisite: FIN 3320. (3-0) Y
