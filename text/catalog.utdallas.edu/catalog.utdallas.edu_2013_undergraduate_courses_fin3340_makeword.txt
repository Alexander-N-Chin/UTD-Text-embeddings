UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogFIN3340 - Regulation of Business and Financial MarketsFIN 3340 Regulation of Business and Financial Markets (3 semester hours) Examines the legal and regulatory environment of business and financial markets. Comparisons between the impact of laws and their original intent are considered, as well as their ethical dimensions. Prerequisite or corequisite: FIN 3320. (3-0) S
