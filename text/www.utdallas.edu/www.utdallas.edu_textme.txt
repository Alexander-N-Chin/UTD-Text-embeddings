UTD Alert


Keep Your Cell Number Current
UT Dallas will send text alerts in the event of an emergency, inclement weather or a disruption in normal University operations. The message will be sent to the mobile telephone number listed in the records of students, faculty and staff members.
If your phone number changes or you are not receiving notifications during tests conducted the first Wednesday of every month at noon, please log into Galaxy and update your contact information.
Parents and others can receive alerts by texting UTDALERT to 888777.
