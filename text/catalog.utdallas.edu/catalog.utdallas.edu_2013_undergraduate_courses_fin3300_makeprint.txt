UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogFIN3300 - Personal FinanceFIN 3300 Personal Finance (3 semester hours) This course is designed to help students prepare for a financially challenging world and to introduce the concepts and methods of personal financial planning. The financial planning process, the time value of money, taxation, credit, budgeting, housing, insurance, and employee benefits will be explored. The course will enable students to manage their finances and develop their own personal financial plans. (3-0) S
