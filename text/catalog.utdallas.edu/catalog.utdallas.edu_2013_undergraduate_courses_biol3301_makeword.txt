UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogBIOL3301 - Classical and Molecular GeneticsBIOL 3301 Classical and Molecular Genetics (3 semester hours) The phenomenon of heredity, its cytological and molecular basis; gene expression and transfer of genetic information, with major focus on bacterial and model eukaryotic systems; genetic recombination and chromosome mapping; tetrad analysis; mutations and mutagenesis; genetic interactions; application of recombinant DNA techniques to genetic analysis. Prerequisites: BIOL 2311 and CHEM 2323 (Organic Chemistry I). Corequisite: BIOL 3101. (3-0) S
