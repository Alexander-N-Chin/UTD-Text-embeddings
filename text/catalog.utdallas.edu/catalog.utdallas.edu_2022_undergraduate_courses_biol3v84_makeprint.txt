UT Dallas 2022 Undergraduate CatalogBIOL3V84 - Clinical Medicine IVBIOL 3V84 Clinical Medicine IV (1-6 semester credit hours) Clinical Medicine IV is a continuation of Clinical Medicine I, II, and III that is offered to students in the UT Partnership in Advancing Clinical Transition (UT PACT) program to be taken during students' third academic year at UT Dallas. Enrollment is limited to students who have completed Clinical Medicine I, II, and III, and at least two years of the UT PACT Program. Credit/No Credit only. UT PACT advisor consent required. Lab fee of $30 required. ([1-6]-[1-9]) Y
