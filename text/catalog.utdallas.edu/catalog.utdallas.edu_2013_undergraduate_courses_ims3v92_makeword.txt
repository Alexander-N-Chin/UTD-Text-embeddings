UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogIMS3v92 - Regional Management Area Studies: Western EuropeIMS 3v92 Regional Management Area Studies: Western Europe (1-3 semester hours) This course familiarizes students with the historical, social, economic, and political background of nations in Europe. Students will learn about the business environment of the area and participate in seminars on firms that operate in and have an economic impact in the area. Prerequisite: IMS 3310. May be repeated for credit (3 hours maximum). ([1-3]-0) R
