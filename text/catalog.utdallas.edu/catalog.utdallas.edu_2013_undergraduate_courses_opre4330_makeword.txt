UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogOPRE4330 - Logistics and Inventory ManagementOPRE 4330 Logistics and Inventory Management (3 semester hours) This course introduces and explains the logistics concepts and systems as well as the related components and managing the inventory in supply chain systems. It also covers the planning, designing and the techniques for managing the distribution of products and services. Prerequisite: OPRE 3310. (3-0) Y
