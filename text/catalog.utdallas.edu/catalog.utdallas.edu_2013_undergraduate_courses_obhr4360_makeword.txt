UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogOBHR4360 - Advanced Organizational Behavior and LeadershipOBHR 4360 Advanced Organizational Behavior and Leadership (3 semester hours) Focus is on the successes and failures of enterprises and the people who run them. We examine the essential elements of leadership in businesses that either lead to sustainable competitive advantage or take the company into crisis and decline. Prerequisites: (OBHR 3310 and OBHR 4350) and (MATH 1326 or MATH 2414 or MATH 2419). (3-0) Y
