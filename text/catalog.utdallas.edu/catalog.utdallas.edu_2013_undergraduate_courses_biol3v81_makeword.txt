UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogBIOL3v81 - Clinical Medicine IBIOL 3v81 Clinical Medicine I (1-6 semester hours) Clinical Medicine is a component of the UT Partnership in Advancing Clinical Transition (UT PACT) program that addresses clinical competencies in the medical profession, including communication skills, professional identity formation, interprofessional teamwork, and medical ethics. Students participate in small group sessions, clinical preceptorships, and hospital rotations at UT Southwestern Medical Center. Enrollment is limited to students who have completed at least one year of the UT PACT Program. May be repeated for credit with permission of UT PACT advisor. ([1-6]-0) Y
