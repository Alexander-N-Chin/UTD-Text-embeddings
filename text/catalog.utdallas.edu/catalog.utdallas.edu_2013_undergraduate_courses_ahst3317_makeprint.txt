UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogAHST3317 - Pioneers of Modern ArtAHST 3317 Pioneers of Modern Art (3 semester hours) Focus on the work of the Post-Impressionists (Seurat, Gauguin, Van Gogh, and Cezanne) and the Symbolists with special emphasis on the artists' contribution to the discourse of ideas and the crisis of meaning in the late 19th century. Prerequisite: ARTS 1301 or equivalent. (3-0) T
