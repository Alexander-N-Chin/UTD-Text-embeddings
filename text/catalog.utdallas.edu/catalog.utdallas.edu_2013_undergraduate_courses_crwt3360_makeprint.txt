UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogCRWT3360 - Art CriticismCRWT 3360 Art Criticism (3 semester hours) This seminar provides a context for practice in the writing of art criticism. Subjects selected for examination may include visual arts, film, dance, theater, music, fiction, and poetry. Prerequisites: ARTS 1301 and CRWT 2301 or equivalent. May be repeated for credit (6 hours maximum). (3-0) R
