UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogOPRE4340 - Purchasing and Sourcing ManagementOPRE 4340 Purchasing and Sourcing Management (3 semester hours) This course introduces and explains the purchasing and sourcing management concepts with focus on selecting, building and managing supplier relationships. It also covers the contract development and its management as well as building the necessary skills for effective negotiation. Prerequisites: OPRE 3310 and (MATH 1326 or MATH 2414 or MATH 2419).
