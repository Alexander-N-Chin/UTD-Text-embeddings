UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogIMS3v91 - Regional Management Area Studies: Latin AmericaIMS 3v91 Regional Management Area Studies: Latin America (1-3 semester hours) This course familiarizes students with the historical, social, economic, and political background of nations in Latin America. Students will learn about the business environment of the area and participate in seminars on firms that operate in and have an economic impact in the area. Prerequisite: IMS 3310. May be repeated for credit (3 hours maximum). ([1-3]-0) R
