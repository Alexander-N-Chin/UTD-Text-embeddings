UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogDANC3340 - Dance in Historical ContextDANC 3340 Dance in Historical Context (3 semester hours) Studies in the history of dance. Topics may include the development of western or world dance forms, specific periods, styles, traditions, and/or artists. May be repeated for credit as topics vary (9 hours maximum). Prerequisite: ARTS 1301 or DANC 1310 or equivalent. (3-0) Y
