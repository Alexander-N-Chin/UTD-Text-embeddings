UT Dallas 2022 Undergraduate CatalogCLDP3362 - Cognitive DevelopmentCLDP 3362 Cognitive Development (3 semester credit hours) Examines the development of children's thinking from birth through adolescence. Topics include theories of cognitive development, language development, memory, social cognition, and the implications of current research in the area of cognitive development. Prerequisite: (CLDP 2314 or PSY 2314) or (CLDP 3310 or PSY 3310) or (CLDP 3339 or PSY 3339) or equivalent. (Same as PSY 3362) (3-0) S
