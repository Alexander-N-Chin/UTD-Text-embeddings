UT Dallas 2020 Undergraduate CatalogCHEM1115 - Honors Freshman Chemistry Laboratory ICHEM 1115 Honors Freshman Chemistry Laboratory I (1 semester credit hour) This course and its follow-on (CHEM 1116) reinforce the concepts of Freshman Chemistry via experiments. Students are offered the opportunity to acquire basic laboratory skills and an appreciation for the presence of chemistry in daily living through a combination of laboratory and computer experiments and applied research modules. Corequisite: CHEM 1315. (0-3) Y
