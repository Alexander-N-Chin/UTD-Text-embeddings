UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogEMAC4372 - Topics in Emerging Media and CommunicationsEMAC 4372 Topics in Emerging Media and Communications (3 semester hours) The course studies fundamental principles and basic techniques of emerging media and communication. Sections may be devoted exclusively to a single aspect of emerging media and communication or to a multiplicity of subjects related to the field. May be repeated for credit as topics vary (12 hours maximum). Prerequisite: Upper-division standing. (3-0) R
