UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogPHYS2125 - Physics Laboratory IPHYS 2125 (PHYS 2125) Physics Laboratory I (1 semester hour) Laboratory course to accompany any Physics I or Mechanics course. Personal computer-based data presentation and curve fitting. Basic measurement concepts such as experimental uncertainty, mean, standard deviation, standard error, and error propagation will be covered. Corequisite: PHYS 1301 or PHYS 2325 or PHYS 2421.
