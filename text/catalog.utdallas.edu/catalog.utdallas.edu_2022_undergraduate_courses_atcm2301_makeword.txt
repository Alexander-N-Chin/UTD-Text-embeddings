UT Dallas 2022 Undergraduate CatalogATCM2301 - Digital Art and Design FoundationsATCM 2301 Digital Art and Design Foundations (3 semester credit hours) This course integrates technical ability with visual communication as it relates to digital art and design. Digital media are prepared for multiple delivery environments, including print, the internet, games, and animation. Students will learn and apply the techniques of digital imaging using tools in the Adobe Creative Cloud software, in concert with foundational knowledge of the elements of art and principles of design. Students will be encouraged to use divergent thinking in problem solving to address emerging media. (0-3) S
