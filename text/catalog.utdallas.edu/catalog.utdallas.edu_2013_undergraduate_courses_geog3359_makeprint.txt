UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogGEOG3359 - Human Migration and Mobility: Global PatternsGEOG 3359 Human Migration and Mobility: Global Patterns (3 semester hours) Explores the nature, structure and geography of population flows, and their influence on evolution of human settlement patterns through the ages. Offers analysis of push and pull factors as reflections of socioeconomic conditions in various regions of the world, and investigates the impacts of globalization on migration and mobility.
