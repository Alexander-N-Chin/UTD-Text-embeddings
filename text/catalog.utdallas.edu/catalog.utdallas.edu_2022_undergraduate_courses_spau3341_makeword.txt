UT Dallas 2022 Undergraduate CatalogSPAU3341 - AudiologySPAU 3341 Audiology (3 semester credit hours) Introduction to clinical application and interpretation in audiology. Topics include basic assessment (e.g., pure-tone audiometry, speech audiometry, basic masking principles), hearing and balance disorders (e.g., diagnosis, evaluation, treatment, and (re)habilitation), as well as special populations and conditions. Prerequisites or Corequisites: SPAU 3304 and SPAU 3344 or instructor consent required. (3-0) Y
