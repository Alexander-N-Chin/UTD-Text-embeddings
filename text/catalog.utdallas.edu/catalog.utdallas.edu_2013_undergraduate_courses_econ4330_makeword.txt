UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogECON4330 - Law and EconomicsECON 4330 Law and Economics (3 semester hours) Contracts, torts, and property rights, integrating economic theory concerning efficiency and equity with actual legal cases. Topics include medical malpractice, habitability laws, zoning, crime deterrence, environmental laws, and discrimination. This course is also recommended for students who are not economics majors.
