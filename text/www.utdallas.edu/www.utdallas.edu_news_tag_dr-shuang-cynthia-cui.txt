Dr. Shuang (Cynthia) Cui
Engineer Aims To Make Manufacturing More Efficient
Feb. 22, 2023

            Dr. Shuang (Cynthia) Cui received a U.S. Department of Energy grant to develop a new method to dry paper in manufacturing that uses up to 60% less energy than the traditional method.
