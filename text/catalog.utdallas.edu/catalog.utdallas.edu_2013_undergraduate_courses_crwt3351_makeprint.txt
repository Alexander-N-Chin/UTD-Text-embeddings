UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogCRWT3351 - Creating PoetryCRWT 3351 Creating Poetry (3 semester hours) A creative workshop on the art of poetry which investigates the creative techniques and processes involved in writing poems in various, often opposing, forms that combine the art of words with the visual and performing arts. May be repeated for credit (9 hours maximum). Prerequisite: CRWT 2301 or instructor consent required. (3-0) T
