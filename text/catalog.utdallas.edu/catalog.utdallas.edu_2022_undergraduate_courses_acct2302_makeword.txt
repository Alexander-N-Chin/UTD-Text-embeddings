UT Dallas 2022 Undergraduate CatalogACCT2302 - Introductory Management AccountingACCT 2302 (ACCT 2302) Introductory Management Accounting (3 semester credit hours) This course helps students to build the necessary skills in the managerial use of accounting information for planning, decision making, performance evaluation, and controlling operations. The course uses a general framework for product costing systems, budgeting and variance analysis in order to benefit all students with a wide variety of career paths. A minimum grade of C is required to take upper-division ACCT courses. Prerequisite: ACCT 2301. (3-0) S
