UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogAHST3315 - The Art of the RenaissanceAHST 3315 The Art of the Renaissance (3 semester hours) Studies in the art and architecture of Italy and/or Northern Europe during the 14th, 15th, and 16th centuries. Special attention is paid to the role of politics, patronage, religious worship, the developing self-consciousness of the artist, and the importance of new techniques. Prerequisite: ARTS 1301 or equivalent. (3-0) T
