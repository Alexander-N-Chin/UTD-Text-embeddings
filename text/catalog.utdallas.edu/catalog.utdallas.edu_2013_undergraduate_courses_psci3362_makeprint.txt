UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogPSCI3362 - The American Political InstitutionsPSCI 3362 The American Political Institutions (3 semester hours) This course examines the constitutional foundations and historical development of the Congress, the presidency, the executive, and the courts. Attention will be paid to both the interactions of these institutions, research methodologies employed in examining these institutions, and the internal workings of each. Prerequisites: GOVT 2301 and GOVT 2302 or instructor consent required. (3-0) Y
