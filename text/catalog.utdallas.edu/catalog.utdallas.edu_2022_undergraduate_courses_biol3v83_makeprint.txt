UT Dallas 2022 Undergraduate CatalogBIOL3V83 - Clinical Medicine IIIBIOL 3V83 Clinical Medicine III (1-6 semester credit hours) Clinical Medicine III is a continuation of Clinical Medicine I and II that is offered to students in the UT Partnership in Advancing Clinical Transition (UT PACT) program, to be taken during students' third academic year at UT Dallas. Enrollment is limited to students who have completed Clinical Medicine I and II, and at least two years of the UT PACT Program. UT PACT advisor consent required. Lab fee of $30 required.
