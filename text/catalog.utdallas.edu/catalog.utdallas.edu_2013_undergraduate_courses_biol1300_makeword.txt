UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogBIOL1300 - Body Systems with LabBIOL 1300 Body Systems with Lab (3 semester hours) Examines the organ systems of mammals, predominantly the human. Function in relation to structure is emphasized. The effects of one organ system on others are stressed. The overall objective of the course is an appreciation of the integration and control of all systems. There is a model-based human anatomy lab. This course is specifically designed for non-majors.
