UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogMIS4342 - Analysis and Design of Accounting SystemsMIS 4342 Analysis and Design of Accounting Systems (3 semester hours) Students are introduced to accounting system analysis and design tools and methods. The course emphasizes business processes, accounting transaction flows, internal control and accounting information systems as part of enterprise systems.
