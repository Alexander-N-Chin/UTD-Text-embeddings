UT Dallas 2022 Undergraduate CatalogBIOL4385 - Oral Histology and EmbryologyBIOL 4385 Oral Histology and Embryology (3 semester credit hours) This course will provide exposure to and broad coverage of maxillofacial and oral histological structures and embryology of the face, neck, and teeth using lectures and electronic images of calcified and soft tissues cells. Prerequisites: (BIOL 3361 and (BIOL 3455 or BIOL 3456)) or instructor consent required. (3-0) S
