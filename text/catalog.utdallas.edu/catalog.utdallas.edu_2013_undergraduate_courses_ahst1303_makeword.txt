UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogAHST1303 - Survey of Western Art History: Ancient to MedievalAHST 1303 (ARTS 1303) Survey of Western Art History: Ancient to Medieval (3 semester hours) An introduction to painting, sculpture, and architecture in the West from prehistory through the late Middle Ages and including the achievements of the ancient Egyptian, Greek, Roman, and Medieval cultures. Monuments will be studied within their historical, religious, and social contexts, with particular focus on the role of art in society and on the development of style. (3-0) Y
