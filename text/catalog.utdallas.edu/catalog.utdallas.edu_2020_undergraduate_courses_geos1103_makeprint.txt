UT Dallas 2020 Undergraduate CatalogGEOS1103 - Physical Geology LaboratoryGEOS 1103 (GEOL 1103) Physical Geology Laboratory (1 semester credit hour) A laboratory to accompany GEOS 1303. The exercises include mineral and rock identification. Topographic maps, geologic maps, and aerial photographs are used to study surface landforms, geologic phenomena and tectonic processes. Prerequisite or Corequisite: GEOS 1303. (0-3) S
