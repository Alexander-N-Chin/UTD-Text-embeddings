UT Dallas 2022 Undergraduate CatalogBMEN1208 - Introduction to Bioengineering IIBMEN 1208 Introduction to Bioengineering II (2 semester credit hours) The purpose of this course is to give students a general understanding of a broad range of applications specific to the biomedical engineering profession. Course exercises include team-oriented project, computer-aided design, introductory materials, and hardware and software tools associated with the discipline. Lab fee of $30 required. Prerequisite: BMEN 1100. Prerequisite or Corequisite: BMEN 1300 or equivalent. (1-2) Y
