UT Dallas 2022 Undergraduate CatalogCS4141 - Digital Systems LaboratoryCS 4141 Digital Systems Laboratory (1 semester credit hour) Laboratory to accompany CS 4341. The purpose of this laboratory is to give students an intuitive understanding of digital circuits and systems. Laboratory exercises include construction of simple digital logic circuits using prototyping kits and board-level assembly of a personal computer. Lab fee of $30 required. Corequisite: CS 4341.
