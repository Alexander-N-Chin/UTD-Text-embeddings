UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogDRAM3310 - Theater/Performance EnsembleDRAM 3310 Theater/Performance Ensemble (3 semester hours) This course is for people who are acting, producing or managing a production. Time will be reserved for rehearsals, script analysis, concept design and general studies. Additional rehearsals, outside of the assigned class time, will be necessary to produce the show. This course provides practical use of theatrical studies. May be repeated for credit as topics vary (9 hours maximum). Prerequisite: Audition or instructor consent required. (0-3) R
