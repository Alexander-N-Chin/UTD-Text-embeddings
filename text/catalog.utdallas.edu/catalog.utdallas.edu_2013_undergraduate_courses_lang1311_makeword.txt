UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogLANG1311 - Beginning Language Instruction ILANG 1311 Beginning Language Instruction I (3 semester hours) This course offers beginning instruction in foreign languages not taught on a regular basis. It integrates acquisition of language skills (listening, speaking, reading, and writing) with study of culture and civilization. Languages will vary but may include Italian, Hindi, or Russian. May be repeated for credit as language varies (6 hours maximum). (3-0) R
