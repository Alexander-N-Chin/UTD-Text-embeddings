UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogBLAW3301 - Employment LawBLAW 3301 Employment Law (3 semester hours) Introduction to the various legal issues that are involved in the modern employment relationship. The topics covered will include employment contracts, employment-at-will, anti-discrimination laws, occupational health and safety, pay equity, employee privacy, and other relevant topics. Prerequisite: BLAW 2301. (3-0) Y
