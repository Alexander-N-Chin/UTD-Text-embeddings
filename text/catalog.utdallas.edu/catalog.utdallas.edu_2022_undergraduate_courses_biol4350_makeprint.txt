UT Dallas 2022 Undergraduate CatalogBIOL4350 - Medical MicrobiologyBIOL 4350 Medical Microbiology (3 semester credit hours) This course will cover the methods used for identification of pathogenic organisms and the study of these organisms in relation to their disease process in humans. We will also cover at the molecular level important concepts such as microbial virulence, the control of bacterial growth, and host responses to infection. Prerequisite: BIOL 3301 or BIOL 3520 or (BIOL 3303 and BIOL 3203). (3-0) R
