UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogBIOL2312 - Introduction to Modern Biology IIBIOL 2312 (BIOL 1307) Introduction to Modern Biology II (3 semester hours) Continuation of BIOL 2311. The overall emphasis will be on organ physiology and regulatory mechanisms involving individual organs and organ systems. Factors considered will be organ development and structure, evolutionary processes and biological diversity, and their effects on physiological mechanisms regulating the internal environment. Corequisite: BIOL 2112. (3-0) S
