UT Dallas 2013 Undergraduate CatalogFIN4335 - Financial Aspects of Retirement and Employee BenefitsFIN 4335 Financial Aspects of Retirement and Employee Benefits (3 semester hours) This course focuses on business and individual retirement plans, planning strategies to meet individual and client goals as well as retirement distribution strategies. Students will evaluate employer and non-employer benefit plans and use a combination of financial planning software to create retirement plans. Prerequisite: FIN 3330. (3-0) Y
